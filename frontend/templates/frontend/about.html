About World Inflation Tracker

World Inflation Tracker is an independent, open-data initiative that tracks consumer prices and calculates inflation rates using internationally recognized methodologies. We started with Sri Lanka as our pilot country, with the architecture designed to expand globally.

Our Mission

  • Provide transparent, independently calculated inflation data
  • Make price data accessible and verifiable
  • Use standardized methodologies comparable to official statistics
  • Maintain full audit trails for every price point

How It Works

Our system scrapes prices daily and weekly from publicly listed e-commerce sites, utility providers, and government sources. These prices feed into a fixed basket of ~85 goods and services, weighted according to household expenditure patterns. The CPI is calculated monthly and rebased to January 2022 = 100.

Limitations

  • Online prices may not always reflect open-market prices for fresh produce
  • Service prices (health, education, transport) often require manual surveying
  • Item substitutions (new models replacing old) are flagged for review
  • Our index is a research tool, not a replacement for official statistics

Disclaimer: This tracker is intended for educational, research, and analytical purposes only.
